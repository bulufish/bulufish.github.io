落日

The story tells about a boy's love, his feelings for a friend, and the bittersweet journey they go through. It explores the actions one takes when longing for someone and the things they use to express that longing.

Story Script

Chen Xing is an introverted boy who has known about his sexuality since childhood, which has made him the target of mockery by his classmates. One day, when Chen Xing was being bullied in a narrow alley, he was saved by Xu Ran.

Xu Ran and Chen Xing are classmates who had no interaction before this incident. However, this incident becomes a turning point in their lives, and they become close friends who share everything. Xu Ran gives Chen Xing a necklace with a symbolic meaning related to his name, as a proof of their friendship.

After school, they walk home together and take the train together. On the train, Xu Ran gets tired and falls asleep, leaning on Chen Xing's shoulder. Chen Xing, who was quietly listening to music with his headphones on, notices and takes off his headphones, silently cherishing their special time together.

On an ordinary day after school, Xu Ran talks to Chen Xing about a girl he likes. This conversation stirs up waves of emotions in Chen Xing's heart, and he realizes that he has feelings for Xu Ran. Chen Xing doesn't immediately express his feelings but silently supports Xu Ran in pursuing the girl he likes.

Feeling dejected, Chen Xing walks home alone. At a street corner, he sees Xu Ran standing across the road, talking happily with the girl. Everything around Chen Xing feels compressed within his sight, making it hard for him to breathe, and his heart races. At that moment, Xu Ran greets Chen Xing from the other side of the road, but Chen Xing doesn't know how to face him and turns and runs away. Just then, a car speeds towards Chen Xing, running a red light. In the critical moment, Xu Ran saves Chen Xing. After the incident, nothing significant happens between them.

After bidding farewell to the girl, Xu Ran walks home with Chen Xing. They reach the riverside where they used to walk together. Xu Ran confesses his feelings to Chen Xing, revealing that he has always liked Chen Xing but didn't know how to express it, which is why he pretended to like the other girl. Chen Xing gladly accepts Xu Ran, and they walk home together. In the glow of the setting sun, the two boys happily walk back.

The story concludes with Chen Xing waking up in the hospital. It turns out that Xu Ran did save Chen Xing at the street intersection, but he couldn't be saved himself and collapsed on the road, covered in blood. Chen Xing witnessed Xu Ran's death and fainted on the spot. When he wakes up, Chen Xing clutches the necklace Xu Ran gave him and bursts into tears repeatedly. Xu Ran can never come back.

Story concept :Jia Yee

True Color

The story tells about a boy's love, his feelings for a friend, and the bittersweet journey they go through. It explores the actions one takes when longing for someone and the things they use to express that longing.

Story Script

This storyboard story was inspired by the song "True Colors" sung by Justin Timberlake and Anna Kendrick.

The story is about a boy who loves drawing but hits a creative block. While recovering in the hospital, he meets a girl who initiates a conversation with him, and they become good friends. The girl helps the boy rediscover his passion for drawing. They draw together and stargaze on the hospital rooftop.

One day, while visiting the girl, the boy accidentally sees her coughing up blood in bed, realizing that her condition has worsened. Despite his worry, he chooses not to share his concerns with the girl and they continue to enjoy their time together. However, gradually, the girl becomes unable to leave her bed. The boy watches her from outside the intensive care unit, separated by a glass window, and makes a decision in his heart.

After a few days, he brings his finished drawing to see the girl, only to find that she is no longer in the hospital room. He rushes to the hospital rooftop where they often spent time, hoping to find her there. However, upon opening the door, he doesn't see the girl. He knows that she has passed away.

Alone on the rooftop, the boy lies down, with his drawing placed beside him. Suddenly, he notices the girl's drawing on the wall. In that moment, the boy accepts the girl's departure. He places his drawing next to hers, depicting her smiling face from his memory.

Story concept :Jia Yee
